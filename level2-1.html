Who Said It Was
Simple

by
Audre Lorde

 There are so many roots to the tree of anger
that sometimes the branches shatter
before they bear.

Sitting in Nedicks                                                      An almost white counterman passes
  • the women rally before they march                   -  a waiting brother to serve them first
  • discussing the problematic girls                         -  and the ladies neither notice nor reject

      they hire to make them free.                                        the slighter pleasures of their slavery.

But I who am

  1. bound

    by my mirror
  2. as well as my bed
    3. see causes in colour
  3. as well as sex
and sit here wondering
which me will survive
all these liberations.
